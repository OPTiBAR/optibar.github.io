About OPTiBAR

OPTiBAR is a collaborative project focused on enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of rebar detailing in concrete structures. We use optimization techniques to streamline the design process and minimize errors.

Our project is open-source, welcoming contributions from engineers, developers, and enthusiasts interested in enhancing the field of structural engineering.
